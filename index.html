FAQ

Frequently Ask Questions

Why pilot in Nepal?

Nepal is one entire country that speaks the same language, in roughly the same dialect. What adds to this advantage is that the Nepali language is well documented making it easy to work with because datasets are accessible.

Healthcare patterns in the country – The majority of the hospitals are in the cities and the towns. There are camps in the country, but these camps are also centred around these areas. Good healthcare doesn’t exist in the country, with people in some regions dying from diseases like Cholera.

How does AI help in this ?

They simply act as doctors, by diagnosing on the basis of whatever is being told to it, just like a normal doctor would. By harnessing the power of cutting-edge natural language processing, patients can talk in their own language in their own way without having to even physically touch the system. Since the input is through speech and the system is finding out the intricacies of the semantics and language used by the patient, no-one has to explain it what the meaning of what is being said or feed it to the computer. Instead, what really happens is that it just understands.

How is this the same as my doctor?

Just like when you visit a doctor, you tell him your symptoms, you visit our platform and talk to it about your symptoms. Just like a doctor then lists out the possible things that might be wrong with you, our machine learning algorithms create a prognosis. If tests need to be carried out for confirmation, just like your doctor, it recommends you to get tested at a nearby clinic. With the test data, it provides you with a diagnosis and a prescription.
